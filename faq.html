Frequently Asked Questions

OUR CHOCOLATE

  • What is a napolitain?

    A napolitain is a tablet of chocolate that weighs 5 grams. Traditionally, a napolitain is served with a cup of coffee!

    Our Maui Grown chocolate is made using 100% cacao grown at our cacao farm in Lahaina.

  • What's the difference between "Maui Grown" chocolate and all your other chocolate?

    "Maui Grown" describes our farm-to-bar Hawaiian chocolate products.

    Our Maui Grown chocolate is made using 100% cacao grown at our cacao farm in Lahaina.

    Our Lahaina cacao farm produces a very limited supply of cacao each year, so we're lucky to source cacao from other unique terroirs around the world.

    Our other chocolate products are made using cacao from an award-winning family-run estate in Costa Esmeraldas, Ecuador.

    Cacao is similar to wine in that the terroir it is grown in is a main contributor to its taste and we are happy to celebrate how diverse cacao can be.

    To learn more about both our Maui and Costa Esmeraldas cacao, please visit our Cacao Origins page!

  • Where is your chocolate made?

    All of our chocolate is made in Hawaii!

    Our Maui Grown chocolate is made using 100% cacao grown at our cacao farm in Lahaina.

  • Where is the cacao sourced from?

    All of our chocolate is made in our Lahaina factory from single-origin cacao!

    Our current chocolate offerings are sourced from either the Maui Ku’ia Estate cacao farm in Lahaina or from Costa Esmeraldas, Ecuador.

    The cacao from our Lahaina farm is used in our Hawaiian Maui Grown chocolate.

    All of our other products use cacao from Costa Esmeraldas, a single-family estate in Ecuador.

    To learn more about our chocolate’s origins, please visit our Cacao Origins page.

  • What's the shelf-life of your chocolate?

    Over time, chocolate has a tendency to bloom, which is when the cocoa butter begins to separate from the cacao solids.

    If you’ve ever seen chocolate turn a bit white around the edges - that’s blooming.

    Our dark chocolate’s best-of date is one year from when it is molded.

    Dark milk's best-of date is one year and a half from when it is molded.

Online Ordering

  • Do you offer gift cards?

    Yes! We offer e-gift cards.

  • What is your return policy?

    In order to protect the public as well as the integrity of our Chocolate Factory, an FDA Certified Food Production facility, we will not be able to accept product returns.

    If there is a problem with your order or concern with our chocolate, we want to hear from you immediately.

    Please email us your order number, name, phone number, a preferred time to reach you, and tell us what happened.

    We will contact you promptly and work with you on the situation.

  • What payment methods are available?

    We accept all major credit and debit cards.

    In our retail store we do not accept cash at this time. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Shipping

  • How long until I receive my order?

    We generally try to send your order within 48 hours of receipt on the days we ship (we currently ship Monday - Thursday).

    Orders received between Thursday morning and Sunday will ship out on Monday at the earliest.

    Through Fedex and UPS, we offer 7-day shipping.

    We will contact you promptly and work with you on the situation.

  • Do you still ship during the warmer summer months?

    Yes! Every order is packed with care in an insulated cool bag with sweat-proof ice packs.

    We prefer to ship Monday - Thursday to avoid having your package sit somewhere hot and unprotected over the weekend.

    During these hotter months, there might be a delay in sending your order.

  • Will your chocolate melt during shipping?

    Each order is packed with care in an insulated cool bag with sweat-proof/non-condensing ice packs to prevent the chocolate from melting.

Allergens & Ingredients

  • Is your chocolate Kosher certified?

    Yes! All of our chocolate is kosher certified.

    Our dark chocolate is Kosher Parve certified and our dark milk chocolate is Kosher Dairy certified.

    Please visit our Kosher Certification page to learn more.

  • Is your chocolate vegan?

    All of our dark chocolate is vegan.

    Our dark milk chocolate does contain dairy.

    We go to great lengths to ensure that milk cannot contact our dark chocolate.

    We run two independent production lines to keep the dark milk chocolate separate from dark chocolate.

  • Is your chocolate gluten-free and soy-free?

    Yes, all of our chocolate is gluten-free and soy-free.

  • Where do the flavors come from?

    Most of our fruit flavors are sourced from fruit picked locally on Maui.

    We grow Mapulehu mangoes on our farm, guavas are handpicked up north in Kapalua.

    Food grade essential oils are available for a variety of plant-based sources and are highly purified by steam distillation.

    We use essential oils in our blood orange, lemongrass, and peppermint chocolate products.

  • Where does the name Ku’ia come from?

    Our cacao farm is located within the ahupua'a named Ku'ia.

    An ahupua'a is how the Hawai'ian people traditionally divided land in triangular form from mountain to sea.

    The ahupua'a system defined a way of life among the people that lived within each specific land division, and the community could only flourish when each individual worked hard and fulfilled the responsibility entrusted upon them.
