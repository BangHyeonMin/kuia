Previous Months

Not sure what to expect? Here are some of our past Ku'ia Club offerings!

  • March 2022

    Experimental Chocolate

    A 48 piece gift box filled with a new version of our 70% Dark 2020 Maui Harvest that was created through inoculated yeast fermentation.

    We used experimental yeast strains that have the ability to develop new possibilities for flavor and aroma.

    Pichia kluyveri is naturally found in New Zealand grape juice and is used by some winemakers to boost fruit flavors.

  • December 2021

    Exclusive Cacao Sources

    A 48 piece gift box filled with two types of dark chocolate: a unique 70% dark chocolate made from cacao sourced from India and a 65% dark chocolate made from a cacao blend sourced from Ecuador and Uganda.

  • August 2021

    OMG

    Our debut Kui'ia Club chocolate of the month box featured a 48-piece gift box and two chocolate bars in our new exclusive flavor Orange, Mango, Guava.

    You'll definitely feel like saying "OMG!" when you try it!
