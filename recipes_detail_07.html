Chocolate Lava Cupcakes Recipe

INGREDIENTS :

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 1/4 c powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 110 grams Maui Ku'ia Chocolate (110 drops or 22 squares)

DIRECTIONS :

  1. Preheat oven to 425 °F and grease cupcake pan.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together (stirring continuously over low heat or in the microwave for about 1 min)
  3. When chocolate and butter are well mixed, mix in sugar.
  4. Mix in eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla.
  5. Stir in flour and mix.
  6. Divide among 8 cupcake wells.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes, remove from oven and let sit for 1 minute. Then serve with ice cream.
