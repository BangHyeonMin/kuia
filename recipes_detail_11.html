Our Holiday Hot Chocolate Recipe

Welcome back to the Maui Ku‘ia Estate Chocolate recipes.

We want send you all our season’s greetings as well as share our recipe for the Hot Chocolate that we plan on serving at our Retail Store at our factory in Lahaina.

HOT CHOCOLATE RECIPE

  • 150 ml (5 oz) Silk brand original version almond milk (60 cal/8 oz serving)
  • 40 g (1.4 oz) 65% dark milk chocolate (Ku‘ia Estate Dark or Dark Milk is the best choice)
  • Measure almond milk into a cylindrical container (large enough for blending), add chocolate in pieces, and heat in microwave to drinking temperature, not boiling, approximately 1 min 20 sec depending on microwave power.
  • Blend using an immersion blender for 10-15 sec. or until smooth. Use pulse mode of blender to avoid liquid overflowing container
  • Transfer to drinking cup of your choice. Double wall stainless steel cups holding 8 oz are the best choice because the hot chocolate stays hot for a long time.

The drink has approximately 200 calories and if using dark chocolate the drink is vegan.

Many flavors of Ku‘ia Estate chocolate will be available to be made into hot chocolate in the retail store.

We invite you to try this recipe at home using whatever chocolate you have and let us know your thoughts.

When our retail store officially opens, join us for a delicious cup of hot chocolate and let us know how ours compares to yours.

Wishing you very Happy Holidays!
