Mole 2 Ways

Cinco de Mayo is just around the corner!

We have not one, but two great mole recipes for you to try at home this year. What a fun new way to eat Maui Ku'ia Estate Chocolate!

Lisa's Homemade Mole

Lisa Foth, our very own Assistant General Manager, is somewhat of a wizard in the kitchen. She uses our Ku'ia Dark Maui Coffee Chocolate to add depth and flavor to this delicious sauce!

This is something worth taking your time to do when creating that special meal for Cinco de Mayo utilizing Maui Chocolate.

INGREDIENTS :

  • Toast & Blister Chiles - Approx. 2 Minutes
  • 8 Dried Mulato Chiles
  • 8 Dried Pasilla Chiles
  • 4 Dried Chilhaucle Negro Chiles
  • 1 Plum Tomato Cut in Half - Remove Skin After Saute
  • 1/2 Medium Onion, Slice into 4 Large Pieces
  • 3 Tomatillos - Remove Husks and Rinse
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • Wipe Pan, Toast and Place in Bowl
  • 3 T. Corn Oil
  • 2 T. Almonds
  • 2T. Peacans
  • 2 T. Peanuts
  • 1 Large Slice Stale Bread Torn into Pieces.
  • 2 Cinnamon Sticks
  • 3 Allspice
  • 3 Cloves
  • 2 T. Raisins
  • 2 T. Pumpkin Seeds or Papitas
  • 2 T. Sesame Seeds
  • 1 t. Dried Oregano
  • 1/8 t. Aniseed
  • 1/8t. Cumin Seed
  • 4 oz. Maui Ku'ia Estate Chocolate Dark Chocolate with Maui Mokka Coffee
  • 4 T. Honey
  • 5 C. Chicken Broth
  • Chile Soaking Liquid
  • Kosher Salt

DIRECTIONS :

  1. Combine All Items (You will need to do this in two batches)
  2. Use either an Immersion Blender or VitaMix to Liquify.
  3. Transfer to Large Dutch Oven to Simmer.
  4. Now it's time to add this to your favorite dishes.

Chef Nabavi's Fig Mole Rojos Sauce

Chef Paris Nabavi is the force behind West Maui's Pizza Paradiso Mediterranean Grill in Honokowai.

You just need one 5g piece of our Dark Chocolate to make this recipe!

INGREDIENTS :

  • ENCHILADA SAUCE = 13
  • Batch Yields = 12 fl oz.- Serves 4
  • 8 floz. Water
  • 1 ea. Tomato quartered
  • 1/4 ea. Onion, chopped
  • 1 ea. Garlic cloves
  • 2 ea. California Chiles
  • 0.5 oz. Butter cubed
  • Salt to taste

DIRECTIONS :

  1. In a stock pot over high heat, bring the water to boil add tomato, onion, garlic and Chiles for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat and let cool.
  3. Blend all the ingredients and strain through a fine mesh sieve.
  4. Press with the back of the ladle to extract all the flavors.
  5. Whisk in the cubed butter and add salt. Refrigerate up to 3 days.

FIG MOLE ROJOS SAUCE

INGREDIENTS :

  • Batch yields = 10 floz
  • 2.5 oz. Peanut Butter
  • 2.5 oz. Pecans
  • 2.5 oz. Pumpkin Seeds
  • 5g 65% Ku'ia Dark Chocolate
  • 1 Tbsp. Cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • 12 floz. Enchilada sauce
  • 10 floz. Hot water
  • 8 floz. Hot water
  • 1 ea. Ancho Chili
  • 2 tsp. Cumin
  • 1.5 oz. Butter
  • Salt to taste

DIRECTIONS :

  1. Heat the enchilada sauce in a large size pot.
  2. Remove the stems and roast Ancho Chiles on the flat top and add to the enchilada sauce, add hot water, peanut butter, pecans, Pumpkin Seeds Sugar and cinnamon and chocolate, let simmer at low heat for 30 minutes, blend all, salt and strain to remove all impurities.
  3. Return to pot add 8-10 oz. water and whisk in the butter in the sauce.
  4. Turn the heat off. Let cool before storing in refrigerator.
